Mayor Patrick Wojahn is running for re-election, and with three other candidates vying for his seat, the mayoral election is the most contested in city history.

Seventeen candidates are running for eight council seats. In each district, at least one incumbent is campaigning to hold on to one of two available seats. Below, we've profiled all 21 candidates and outlined voter history for those with council experience

The elections will take place on Nov. 7 between 7 a.m. and 8 p.m. Residents of Districts 1 and 4 can vote at Davis Hall (9217 51st Ave), and residents of Districts 2 and 3 can vote at City Hall (4500 Knox Road).
